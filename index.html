Our Story

Inspired by Tradition

Founded in 2005, Mana Oori Ruchulu was born from our passion to bring authentic village flavors to the city. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional cooking methods that give our dishes their distinctive taste.

Our Philosophy

We believe in honoring the rich culinary heritage of Indian villages. Every dish we serve embodies the essence of rural India - simple, hearty, and bursting with authentic flavors. We source our ingredients from local farmers, supporting sustainable practices while ensuring the freshest produce for our customers.
